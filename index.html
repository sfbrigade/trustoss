Open source projects are built on trust.

Trust between maintainers and contributors,
between users and programmers,
and between community partners and development teams.

As trust is built around a project, adoption and community grow.

TrustOSS is a proposed documentation standard built around the goal of building and maintaining trust in your projects.

In addition to the documention standard, a command-line utility is being developed which will help:
bootstrap project docs and
lint the health of your current docs.

Take a look at the philosophy, view the spec, or install the essential files.
